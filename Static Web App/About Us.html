At Harry's Pottery Store, we have more than 40 years of experience making pottery by hand. Our goal is to bring you joy when you purchase one of our pottery by providing you with the best quality of pottery at an affordable and reasonable price.

Our History

On July 31, 1990, Harry D. R. Cliffe opened Harry's Pottery Store in Clay County, North Carolina. Our original store was located at the intersection of Kiln Street and Silica Road. On June 26, 1997, we moved to our current location on Phil Stone Avenue and have been here ever since.

Harry's Pottery Store has been family owned since 1990.
